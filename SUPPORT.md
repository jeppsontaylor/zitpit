# Support Policy

As a community-driven project, ZitPit is supported by contributors on a best-effort basis.

## Community Help

*   **GitHub Issues**: For bugs and feature requests. Please use the [ISSUE_TEMPLATE](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE).
*   **Discord/Slack**: (Placeholder) Join our community chat for real-time discussion.

## Commercial Support

Currently, ZitPit does not offer official commercial support. If you are an enterprise looking for a managed version or custom implementation, please reach out via our [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) contact.

## Feature Requests

If you need a specific adapter (npm, Cargo, etc.) and it's not yet on our [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md), we encourage you to:
1.  Discuss it in an Issue.
2.  Help implement it!
