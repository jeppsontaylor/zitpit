# ZitPit Research & Publications

This directory houses the academic and technical research behind the **ZitPit** defense architecture. We welcome collaboration from researchers in the fields of **Supply-Chain Security**, **Isolation & Deception**, and **Agentic Software Engineering**.

## Our Goal

Our mission is to publish a definitive paper on "Deception-First Defenses for Agentic Development." We believe that the current model of direct internet access for AI agents is fundamentally broken and requires a new, high-performance, and verifiable trust perimeter.

If you are an academic or researcher, we invite you to:
1.  Review our threat model and research goals.
2.  Suggest improvements or additional research areas.
3.  Co-author academic papers with the ZitPit community.
